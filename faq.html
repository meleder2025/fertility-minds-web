The basics

What is Fertility Minds?

Fertility Minds is a wellness and community app for people on a fertility journey. It brings together expert-led meditations, video sessions, articles and moderated peer communities — all designed to support the emotional side of trying to conceive, treatment, loss or supporting a partner.

Who is the app for?

It's for anyone navigating fertility — whether you're just starting to try, in active treatment, coping with loss, supporting a partner, or exploring alternative routes to parenthood. We have content and community spaces for every chapter.

Is Fertility Minds a medical service?

No. Fertility Minds is a wellness and community app. Our content is reviewed by qualified fertility specialists but it is not a substitute for medical advice. For medical questions about your fertility, please speak to your GP or fertility clinic.

When is the app launching?

Fertility Minds is launching soon on the Apple App Store and Google Play. Get in touch to be notified the moment we go live.

What devices does it work on?

Fertility Minds will be available on iOS (iPhone and iPad) and Android phones and tablets at launch. Minimum supported versions and exact requirements will be published on the app store listings.

Subscription & billing

Is there a free version?

Yes. You can use core features of Fertility Minds free of charge, including mood check-ins, selected content and community access. Premium unlocks the full library of expert-led wellness content and unlimited community features.

How much does Premium cost?

Premium is £7.99 per month, billed monthly through your Apple App Store or Google Play account. There are no commitments — you can cancel any time. Pricing in other regions may vary based on local currency and store conversions.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the app store you subscribed via:

  • iOS: Settings › your name › Subscriptions › Fertility Minds › Cancel Subscription.
  • Android: Google Play app › menu › Subscriptions › Fertility Minds › Cancel.

For full details see our Subscription Terms.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds for App Store and Google Play subscriptions are handled by Apple and Google respectively. See our Refund Policy for details and a step-by-step guide on how to request one.

Privacy & community

Is my data safe?

Yes. Fertility Minds is fully GDPR-compliant and built with privacy as a core principle. We never sell your data. You have full control over what you share, what's public in community spaces, and what stays private. See our Privacy Policy for the complete detail.

Can I post anonymously in the community?

Yes. You can choose to post or comment anonymously in any community space, so you can share at the pace that feels right for you.

How is the community moderated?

Our community team actively moderates all groups against our Community Guidelines. We use a combination of automated tools and human review to keep the space safe, respectful and supportive.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account directly in the app under Settings › Privacy › Delete Account, or via our account deletion page. Deletion is permanent and removes your personal data within 30 days.
