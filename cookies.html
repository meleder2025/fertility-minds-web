Cookies Policy

A short, honest policy. We use only essential cookies on this website.

Last updated: 20 May 2026

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work or work more efficiently, and to provide information to site operators.

2. What we use on this website

This website (fertilityminds.com) uses only essential storage — specifically, a single localStorage entry to remember that you've dismissed the cookie notice. We do not use:

  • Advertising or marketing cookies
  • Third-party tracking cookies
  • Analytics cookies (e.g. Google Analytics)
  • Social media tracking pixels

3. Inside the mobile app

The Fertility Minds mobile app uses standard device storage to keep you signed in and remember your preferences. The app does not use third-party advertising trackers. For full detail on what data the app collects and how it is used, please read our Privacy Policy.

4. Managing storage in your browser

You can clear or block cookies and local storage at any time in your browser settings. Doing so will not affect your ability to use this website.

5. Changes to this policy

If we ever introduce additional cookies (for example, optional analytics in future), we will update this policy and ask for your consent first.

6. Contact

Questions? Email hello@fertilityminds.com.
